About Us

Insurance Agent Ram

LIC (Life Insurance Corporation of India) insurance agent known for his expertise in the field of life insurance and financial planning. As an LIC agent, his primary responsibilities include selling life insurance policies, advising clients.

LIC holds a significant share of the Indian life insurance market, with a large customer base and widespread reach across the country. the company's extensive network includes over 2,000 branches and more than 1.2 million agents. Life Insurance Corporation of India (LIC) is an Indian multinational Central Public sector life insurance company headquartered in Mumbai. It is India's largest insurance company as well as the largest institutional investor with total assets under management worth ₹52.52 trillion (US$630 billion) as of March 2024.
